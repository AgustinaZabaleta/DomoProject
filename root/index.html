The action of the set of home automation installations has repercussions on an intelligent management of the home, providing a series of benefits related to comfort, security, greater energy efficiency, as well as an improvement in communication, since we have the possibility of controlling our home from anywhere outside using our mobile device. What we offer

Wired products

Very efficient and having stability due to the speed with which the signals are transmitted, as well as the absence of obstacles for the information to reach the corresponding equipment correctly.

Wireless

The information is transmitted between transmitters and receivers through radiofrequency waves that interpret the different orders. The great advantage of wireless systems is that they can be incorporated to any type of project, whether it is a new construction or one already completed.

Mixed

In mixed systems, a single control is used that is responsible for exchanging information with the different devices.
